## Regulatory News
EU Finalizes Comprehensive Crypto Regulation Framework
The European Union has finalized its Markets in Crypto-Assets (MiCA) regulation, establishing a comprehensive framework for cryptocurrency operations within the bloc. The new rules, which will take effect in 2024, include provisions for stablecoin issuers, crypto service providers, and consumer protection measures. Industry participants have generally welcomed the clarity, though some express concerns about compliance costs for smaller entities.
